How Nofence works

Before we ask about your herd, here's a quick overview of what a Nofence setup includes.

📡
GPS Virtual Fence
You draw the grazing zone on the Nofence app. The collar gently warns animals approaching the boundary with a sound — no physical fence needed.
📱
Nofence App
Monitor every animal's location in real time, set up alerts, move pasture zones, and manage your subscription — all from your phone.
☀️
Solar-powered collars
Each collar charges itself in daylight. For shaded or heavily forested land, we'll recommend spare batteries to keep your setup running year-round.
