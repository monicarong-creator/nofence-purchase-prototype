Let's see if you need shelter beacons with your set-up

Barns and other buildings can interfere with GPS signals. When signals bounce off a roof or walls, a collar may log an inaccurate position. Shelter beacons help stabilise the GPS signal inside or near shelters.

Yes — I use barns, buildings, or shelters
Animals go inside regularly (overnight, winter housing, etc.)
Shelter Beacon
To ensure the system works properly, you will need to install a few shelter beacons.
The shelter beacon range is about 16 feet (5 m). In larger rooms or open barns, we recommend using at least two shelter beacons for full coverage. One beacon per standard-sized building is usually sufficient.
